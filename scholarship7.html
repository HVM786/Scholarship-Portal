MAHE Manipal awards Merit Scholarships to meritorious students admitted under GENERAL category to
  • Manipal Law School (MLS), MAHE Bengaluru

Merit Scholarship for the candidates joining UG programs

  • All Candidates who fulfill the following criteria are eligible for Merit Scholarship
    • Category Aggregate Percentage in 10+2 OR CLAT AIR Scholarship Subsequent Years: Performance-based
    Merit Scholarship ≥ 95 %, < 100 % OR Upto AIR 1000 30% UG: Pass in all subjects
    ≥ 85 %, < 95 % OR AIR: 1001–2000 20%
    ≥ 75 %, < 85 % OR AIR: 2001–3000 10%
  • The quantum of Merit scholarship as per academic performance is mentioned above
  • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee including Refundable Caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000 at the time of Counseling / Admission.
  • The Candidates admitted under the Merit Scholarship category will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books, transport etc.
  • After due verification, the list of students eligible for MAHE Merit Scholarships will be finalized and MAHE Admissions Office will email the MAHE Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the eligible candidates
  • The excess fee paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded within 2 months of class commencement, only on submission of duly filled Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the MAHE Bengaluru Admissions office.
  • The award of Merit scholarship for the subsequent years will be same as given at the time of admission
  • Scholarship will be extended in the subsequent years only to those students who have cleared all subjects in the University Examinations of the previous year before the due date for payment of annual course fee.
  • Students, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, will NOT be eligible for scholarship for that academic year.
  • The scholarships will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc.
  • In case the Scholar leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal
