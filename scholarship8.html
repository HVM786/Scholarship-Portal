Scholarship Guidelines

  • Minimum Eligibility Requirements
      • Pass in BE / BTech in a relevant branch from a recognized University, with minimum 50 % aggregate marks or equivalent
      • The candidate MUST be having the Manipal Entrance Test (MET) Rank for MTech / ME program
  • The scholarship involves a 20 % waiver of the Course fee for the candidates admitted to the program under the GENERAL category
      • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee including refundable Caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000 before the deadline specified for payment of full first year fee.
      • The Candidates admitted under this category will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc
  • The excess fee paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded after the Class commencement
  • The award of scholarship for the subsequent years will be same, as awarded during year 1.
  • Continuation of scholarship for subsequent years, will be based on academic performance of the students at the University examinations preceding the due date for payment of fee / dues i.e. Minimum CGPA of 7 in a 10-point grading system
      • Students, who are promoted to next year with less than 7 CGPA, are NOT eligible for scholarship and MUST pay the full Course Fee applicable for that academic year
      • Students, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, are NOT eligible for scholarship and MUST pay the full Course Fee applicable for that academic year
      • Scholarships will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc
  • In case the student leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the full Course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal
  • Note Students enrolled in the program under WILP are not eligible for this scholarship The scholarship is applicable for the fee payable towards the Course Fee for MAHE Manipal
